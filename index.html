Visit Tirukazhukundram

Enjoy the culture, spirituality and scenic beauty of this town near Chengalpattu

Top three things to do at Tirukazhukundram

Visit Periya Koyil

Start your spiritual journey at the temple of Lord Vedagiriswarar and Thiripurasundari Amman

Visit Malai Koyil

Climb all the 600 steps to worship the diety on the mountain top and enjoy the serene beauty of the town

Take a dip in the temple tank

Sangutheertha Kulam is one of the largest temple tanks in Tamil Nadu. Take a holy dip and wash your sins away.
